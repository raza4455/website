Halwa Recipe

I will make Halwa in a good way and you will also see with me

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sooji
  • 1/3 cup Ghee
  • 1/4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • Almonds (Badam)
  • Cashew nuts
  • teaspoon Cardamom powder

Method

  1. Pour 1/4 cups water in a small saucepan and bring it to boil over medium flame
  2. Take sooji in a heavy based pan, add ghee and heat it over medium flame.
  3. Stir and mix using spatula.
  4. Roast inn until golden-brown. it will take anywhere between 8 to 10 minutes.
  5. Reduce flame to low; slowly add boiled water and mix by stirring constantly.
  6. Stir and cook to avoid any lump formation.
  7. Increase flame to medium and cook until all water gets absorbed. Add sugar and Cardamom powder.
  8. Stir until sugar dissolves and it has s semi-solid consistency. Add and mix almonds and cachew nuts after reserving some of them for garnishing.
  9. The "Sooji Halwa" is now ready for serving.
